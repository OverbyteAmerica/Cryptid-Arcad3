EQUIFAX

Incident Overview

Equifax failed to patch a known Apache Struts vulnerability. Once compromised, attackers encountered minimal segmentation and weak policy enforcement, allowing unrestricted lateral movement.

CIA Triad Impact

Confidentiality
PII exposure at national scale.
Integrity
Data validation impossible post-breach.
Availability
Operational shutdowns during response.

Attack Path Visualization

MITRE ATT&CK Mapping

Initial Access Exploitation of Public-Facing Application (T1190)
Lateral Movement Internal Spearphishing & Credential Abuse (T1021)
Collection & Exfiltration Automated Data Collection (T1119)

Recommended Remediations

Network Segmentation Implement strict zone isolation between public, application, and data layers.
Policy Administration Enforce least-privilege access and continuous access reviews.
Monitoring & Detection Deploy behavioral analytics and anomaly detection.
